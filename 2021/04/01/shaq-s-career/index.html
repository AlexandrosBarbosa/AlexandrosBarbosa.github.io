Shaq's Career

2021-04-01

When in Shaq’s Career did, he Perform his best and how did he do it?

Shaq’s one of the most dominant player ever in the NBA who started his career in Orlando.Shaq’s career really took off when he joined the Lakers where he would win three championships and later in his career he would join the Miami heat and win one more championship. So how did he do it on the court? We are going to Look at Shaq’s Career and when did he perform his best and how he did it offensively and defensively.

Next we are going to look at some of Shaq’s career stats in a waffle chart like Free throws attempts and Field goal attempts and other stats and get a more of visual how he impacted on court compared to other ways.

The first step we are going to have our library and waffle since and put in the variables from our chart and the amount and to adjust amount to our rows.

library(waffle)
## Warning: package 'waffle' was built under R version 4.0.4
log <- c("Free throws attempts"=5626, "Field goal attempts"=9728)
log <- c("Free throws attempts"=5626, "Field goal attempts"=9728)
waffle(log/20, rows = 20, xlab="1 square = 1 FTA or FGA", colors = c("black", "blue", "white")) + labs(title="Free throws attempts vs. Field goal attempts", subtitle="Shaq's Free throw attempts are nearly half of his Field goals attempts", caption="Source: Sports Statistics | Graphic by Alex Barbosa") + 
  theme(
    plot.title = element_text(size = 16, face = "bold"),
    axis.title = element_text(size = 10),
    axis.title.y = element_blank()
  )        

log<- c("Blocks"=2732, "Steals"=739)
waffle(log/10, rows = 14, xlab="1 square = 1 Block/Steal", colors = c("black", "blue", "white")) + labs(title="Blocks vs. Steals", subtitle="Shaq defensively impacted more by getting blocks compared to steal", caption="Source: Sports Statistics | Graphic by Alex Barbosa") + 
  theme(
    plot.title = element_text(size = 16, face = "bold"),
    axis.title = element_text(size = 10),
    axis.title.y = element_blank()
  )      

log<- c("Offensive Rebounds"=2104, "Defensive Rebounds"=4445)
waffle(log/10, rows = 14, xlab="1 square = 1 Offensive Rebounds/Defensive Rebounds", colors = c("black", "blue", "white")) + labs(title="Offensive Rebounds vs. Defensive Rebounds ", subtitle="Shaq collected more Defensive Rebounds over his career", caption="Source: Sports Statistics | Graphic by Alex Barbosa") + 
  theme(
    plot.title = element_text(size = 16, face = "bold"),
    axis.title = element_text(size = 10),
    axis.title.y = element_blank()
  )

The Chart shows us that in Shaq’s career Free throws attempts were indeed a huge part in chances for Shaq to score but with that we expected Field goal attempts to be more but free throws were pretty measurable opportunities for shaq to score compared to Field goal attempts.

The next chart we are looking at block vs. Steals and see base on the chart the Shaq’s defensive impacted in his career more by blocking opponents more compare to getting steals from his opponents.

The last chart we see offensive Rebounds vs. Defensive Rebounds and that Shaq got more Defensive Rebounds over his career but Shaq collected decent amount of offensive Rebounds compared to Defensive Rebounds.
